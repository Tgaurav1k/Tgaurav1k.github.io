Privacy Policy

PayTrack (Expense Tracker) · Last updated: 10 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the PayTrack mobile application (also listed as Expense Tracker, package com.paytrack.app) collects, uses, and safeguards your information. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Summary in plain words

  • All your financial data stays on your device. We do not have a server that stores your transactions.
  • The app reads bank and UPI SMS only on your phone, only to detect transactions for your own ledger.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not share transaction data with anyone.
  • The only data that leaves your device is the email you enter at sign-up (used to send you a one-time verification code) and anonymous advertising identifiers used by Google AdMob.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Name, email address, phone number — entered during sign-up. Stored locally on your device.
  • Email address — sent to our email-delivery service so we can send you a 6-digit one-time password (OTP) to verify your account.
  • Profile photo (optional) — stored locally on your device.
  • Transactions, budgets, recurring payments, split bills, day notes — stored locally on your device only.

2.2 Information accessed via Android permissions

  • SMS (READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS) — the app scans incoming SMS for bank/UPI transaction patterns (debit/credit alerts, UPI confirmations) so it can automatically add them to your ledger. SMS content is parsed entirely on your device. Raw SMS text is never transmitted to us or to any third party. You can use the app fully without granting this permission — manual entry and screenshot-based entry remain available.
  • Camera — used only when you choose to scan a QR code or capture a receipt. Images are processed on-device and not uploaded.
  • Photos / Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES) — used when you choose a profile photo or a receipt image from your gallery. The app accesses only the file you explicitly pick.
  • Biometric (USE_BIOMETRIC, USE_FINGERPRINT) — used for the optional App Lock feature. Biometric matching is handled by the Android operating system; the app never receives your fingerprint or face data.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — used to send you daily expense digests and budget alerts that you have opted into.
  • Exact alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM) — used to deliver your daily digest at a precise time chosen by you.
  • Boot receiver (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED), Foreground service — used to re-arm scheduled local notifications and the background SMS scan after a device reboot.
  • Internet — used only to (a) send you OTPs over email and (b) load Google AdMob ads.

2.3 Information collected automatically

  • Advertising identifier & ad-related data — Google AdMob (a Google service) collects an Android Advertising ID, approximate location derived from IP, device model, and similar information for the purpose of showing ads. AdMob is governed by Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. How we use information

  • To verify your account at sign-up via a one-time email code.
  • To populate your local ledger with transactions detected from SMS or entered manually.
  • To deliver budget alerts, daily digests, and other notifications you have enabled.
  • To display ads through Google AdMob.

4. Data storage and retention

All ledger data — transactions, budgets, recurring payments, split bills, day notes, profile information — is stored only on your device in a local SQLite database. We do not maintain a server-side copy.

You can delete all of your data at any time by tapping Profile → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes the local database and signs you out. Uninstalling the app also removes all on-device data.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, except:

  • With our transactional email provider, solely to deliver the one-time verification code to your email address at sign-up.
  • With Google AdMob, which receives advertising identifiers as described in Section 2.3.
  • Where required by law, court order, or other lawful request from a competent authority.

6. Children

PayTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your choices

  • You can deny or revoke any Android permission from the system settings without uninstalling the app.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising from your device’s Ad Settings.
  • You can delete your account and all local data from Profile → Account → Delete Account.

8. Security

All data is stored locally and protected by your device’s OS-level sandboxing. The optional App Lock adds biometric authentication on top of this. Network calls (OTP delivery, ads) use HTTPS.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
